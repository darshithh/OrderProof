How OrderProof Works

An intelligent multi-signal engine to combat delivery refund fraud.

OrderProof operates at the interface between customer service and risk management. When a customer requests a refund citing unhygienic substances in their food (such as hair, insects, lizard, plastic), this system automatically analyses the submission to compute a multi-layered risk score.

1

Image & Filename Forensic Analysis

Scans file names for AI generation signatures and verifies JPEG EXIF camera metadata to detect internet downloads or mockups.

2

Perceptual Hash & Duplicate Detection

Hashes image content (SHA-256) to ensure the exact same image hasn't been recycled from an old customer complaint.

3

Customer & Restaurant Cross-Referencing

Looks up order delivery times (detecting late complaints) and analyzes historical reports for claim repetition or restaurant corroboration.
